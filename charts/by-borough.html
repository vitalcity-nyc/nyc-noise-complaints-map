Sound and the city

The Bronx and Manhattan call 311 about noise the most, per person

Manhattan files the most noise complaints overall, but the Bronx is right behind it. Adjust for population and a different picture emerges: Manhattan and the Bronx are each more than five times as complaint-heavy as Staten Island, per resident.

Sources: New York City 311 Service Requests (dataset erm2-nwe9), filtered to complaint types beginning with "Noise"; borough populations from the American Community Survey 2023 one-year estimates. Total counts include only complaints filed at addresses with 10 or more noise complaints in the period (82 percent of the citywide total); per-capita rates are calculated on that same set. Window: January 1, 2025 through May 1, 2026.
