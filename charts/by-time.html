Sound and the city

Every kind of noise has its own rhythm

Commercial noise peaks at night. House of worship peaks in the morning. Helicopters are almost never reported after dark. Each row shows what share of that category's complaints arrive in each block of the day.

Source: New York City 311 Service Requests (dataset erm2-nwe9), filtered to complaint types beginning with "Noise." Each bar is that category's share of complaints in the local-time block. Time blocks: morning 6 a.m. to noon, afternoon noon to 6 p.m., evening 6 p.m. to midnight, late night midnight to 6 a.m. Window: January 1, 2025 through May 1, 2026.
