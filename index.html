Methodology and notes

Source: NYC 311 Service Requests dataset (erm2-nwe9) on NYC Open Data. Filter: complaint_type begins with "Noise". Date field: created_date.

What's a "chronic" address? A point on the map that has received at least the threshold number of complaints in the selected time window, after rounding lat/lng coordinates to roughly 50-meter blocks (so closely-spaced addresses are merged).

Helicopter caveat: Helicopter noise complaints are filed against an address but the helicopter is rarely overhead — many cluster at LaGuardia, JFK, and Manhattan heliports rather than where residents heard it.

What this map is not: a measurement of how loud New York is. It is a measurement of where and when residents called 311. Reporting rates vary by neighborhood, and many noise events go unreported.
